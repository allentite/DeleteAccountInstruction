Delete Account Instruction

Last updated: December 25, 2023

 

If you want to delete your account, please send an email to support@volktek.com with your account details and a request for account closure. Be sure to use the email associated with your account for verification purposes.

Once your email is received and processed, you should receive a confirmation message indicating that your account deletion request is being handled. Further instructions or confirmation may be provided through email correspondence with the support team.
